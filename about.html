ABOUT COVID RELIEF FUND ORGANISATION

The COVID-19 pandemic is one of the worst health and economic crises in modern history and it continues to require the best of humanity to overcome.Covid Relief Fund is a organization, where the volunteers can donate certain amount of money for the covid affected patients which will be useful in many aspects. Your donation to this fund will help stop the spread of the virus around the world and your donation can help the covid patients or the front line workers to afford for their food, oxygen supplies, medications etc..
